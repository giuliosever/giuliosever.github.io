Welcome! I am Giulio Severijnen, a cognitive neuroscientist/psycholinguist. Currently, I work as a postdoctoral researcher at the [International School for Advanced Studies (SISSA)](https://www.sissa.it/), in the [Language, Learning, and Reading Lab](https://lrlac.sissa.it/) led by prof. Davide Crepaldi. I am currently studying the underlyling (neural) mechanisms of statistical learning and reading.

Previously, I was a PhD candidate at the [Donders Institute for Brain, Cognition, and Behaviour](https://www.ru.nl/en/donders-institute) and the [Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics](https://mpi.nl/), working with dr. Hans Rutger Bosker, dr. Ashley Lewis, and prof. James McQueen. During my PhD, I examined how listeners deal with acoustic varability in prosody through perceptual learning and rate normalization. 

To learn more about my research, you can have a look at my [publications](https://giuliosever.github.io/publications/).
